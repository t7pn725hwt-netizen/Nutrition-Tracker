Weekly Insights

🔥 Post-OTF Banana Dose

Underdosed all 4 OTF sessions. 2u peaked to 155–189 every time. OTF cortisol suppresses insulin sensitivity for ~2 hours post-session. Target 4–5u for banana+shake on OTF days. This is the clearest repeatable pattern in the week.

✓ Pizza 12u — Confirmed

Three clean pizza days (May 13, 16, 18). Pure pizza days peaked max 173, zero time above 180. The May 13 spike was wings+pizza stacking — not pizza alone. 12u for pepperoni pizza is locked. Never adjust down.

⚠ BBQ / High-Fat Meals

Most variable meal of the week. Same 77g carb BBQ thighs meal ranged from 126 peak (11u, May 16) to 216 peak (13u, May 15). Pre-meal BG and fat content drive the gap. BBQ beans + cornbread (May 19) needed 10u+ for 44g, not 7u — cornbread is extremely fast-absorbing.

⚠ Stacking & Hypos

4 of 5 hypo events were Humalog stacking: May 13 (double hypo 65/62), May 15 (nadir 63), May 16 (nadir 60). All involved <3hr between doses with IOB still active. Minimum 3hr between Humalog doses when pre-dose BG is under 120.

✓ May 18 — Perfect Template

100% TIR, avg 122. Pattern: 2 Humalog doses only, no stacking, no high-glycemic uncertainty. Pizza 12u → clean. Salad 4u from BG 99 → peak 144. Simple meal structure = predictable glucose. Replicate this on non-OTF days.

➙ Correction Doses

Six corrections this week (May 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 18, 19). All were 1–2u on BG 172–221 — systematically too small. At typical ISF, BG 215 with zero IOB needs 3–4u. Every undercorrection extended above-180 time by 1–2 hours and contributed to the following hypo risk.

Fiber Gap

Missed 4/7 days (May 13, 14, 17, 18). Avocados = primary fiber source. Days with 2 avocados all hit or exceeded 30g. On avocado-free days (May 18: 26g), fiber fell short. Consider adding chia seeds or psyllium on low-avocado days.
